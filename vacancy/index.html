About Brij

Brij is a VC backed, high-growth tech startup powering the future of connected commerce with the simple scan of a QR code or NFC tag. Brij is on a mission to empower brands to build stronger relationships with their customers by connecting physical products to personalized digital experiences.

Customers buy a product, scan the tag, and instantly get discounts, rewards, exclusive content and transparency. We are creating an entirely new marketing channel. *We have a product out to market and both live and paying customers.

The Team

Brij was founded by two Harvard Business School graduates who connected over their shared vision for smarter & connected products. Kait, CEO and Co-Founder, has deep retail and commerce experience. Zack, CTO and Co-Founder, is the tech and product lead, with a MS from Carnegie Mellon and a background in product development, design and manufacturing.

They are joined by a team of passionate individuals focused on engineering, design, sales and marketing. We are mission-driven, customer-focused, and detail oriented.

In your first 30 days
  • Understand existing code base (meteor/angular) and have a working knowledge of building and deploying code base
  • Work with contractor team to fix any high priority issues in existing codebase and help launch the product go live
  • Work on refactoring (and maybe adding simple features) some of the code in existing codebase
  • Work with team to help come up with strategy, identify a timeline to gradually migrate code to a more modern stack
  • Design, architect, and write proof of concepts to test out various hypothesis
